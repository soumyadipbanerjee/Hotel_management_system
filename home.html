About

Hotel Banerjee Woodland is located in the heart of Chittaranjan in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of tress, churches, buildings, shops and monuments. Banerjee Woodland Hotel is part of a lovingly restored 180 palace. On entering this charming hotel in Chittaranjan, you will immediately sense its special intimate atmosphere that makes you feel like being in your own florentine home. Each detail has been passionately chosen and each room deserves a visit. Hotel Banerjee Woodland style mixes valuable antiques and original artworks with an unexpected eclectic contemporary twist.
